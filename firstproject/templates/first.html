ABOUT US

Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering for Women (GVPCEW) is a very well-known Science and Engineering college. This college is started in 2008 and located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. GVPCEW provides a technologically focused education for undergraduate and graduate students in fields ranging from Engineering, Computing and Sciences. The missions of GVPCEW provide advanced knowledge and educate students in Science and technology and also teaching research, and innovation strong academic work ethic at Tech is balanced by an intellectual environment incorporating both inter-college and in-house sports, campus culture.
